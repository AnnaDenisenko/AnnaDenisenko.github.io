Welcome! I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Harris School of Public Policy at the University of Chicago and previously served as a Postdoctoral Associate at the W. Allen Wallis Institute of Political Economy at the University of Rochester. I recently completed my PhD in Politics at New York University. My research focuses on political economy, where I use game-theoretic models to examine situations where private incentives of the agents run counter those of their principals.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, climbing, and practicing piano and melodica.
